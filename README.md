The story contained within my art may speak to you now, or possibly 
it's a story you've yet to experience. Visual art has a way of touching 
your soul in ways that words cannot.

I make art that brings out memories and emotions because we all need to 
remember what makes us human. In a world of AI, we need more art that 
is thought provoking, riveting, and poignant — not just another carbon 
copy or "it looks nice but I've seen it before."

I'm Lauren, and I create painterly, textured, organic style art. I focus 
on keeping things human and handmade because we need to not abandon what 
makes us human.
